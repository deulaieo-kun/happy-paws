ABOUT US

Happy Paws is a non-profit organization established to provide a safe haven for stray dogs and others who do not have a permanent home and/or fur parents. In order to maintain enough funds for its operations, they also engage in selling dog products such as foods and accessories. All in all, it has helped many of our fur pals who now have a place where they actually belong.
